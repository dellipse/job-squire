{# ══════════════ ai ══════════════ #} {% elif step_key == 'ai' %}

AI powers Job Squire's best features: scoring new jobs against your profile, tailoring resumes and cover letters per application, drafting follow-up emails, and weekly strategy reviews. Three ways to get it, in order of privacy:

1. Local AI (most private). Run Ollama on your own machine — your data never leaves it. Needs a reasonably capable computer (16 GB+ memory recommended). Add it under Settings → AI → Providers with provider type "Ollama (local)".
2. Cloud AI with free tiers. Google Gemini, Groq, and OpenRouter all offer free API tiers that work well here. Your identifiers are automatically replaced with placeholders before anything is sent (see Privacy settings). Add keys under Settings → AI → Providers.
3. A Claude subscription (Pro/Max). Connect Claude directly to Job Squire as a live connector — Claude reads your pipeline and writes analyses, kits, and drafts back on its own. Set up under Settings → AI → Connectors. Also have a ChatGPT, Gemini, or Nous subscription? Their API keys work via the Providers tab too.

{# ══════════════ notifications ══════════════ #} {% elif step_key == 'notifications' %}

Job Squire can email you search digests, follow-up reminders, and the weekly strategy review — sent from your own instance to your own inbox, not bulk mail to strangers. That matters for deliverability: this is exactly the low-volume, single-recipient case that free email providers handle well without tripping spam filters.

{% if smtp.enabled and smtp.host %}

{% endif %}
1. Recommended: SMTP2GO. Free forever, no credit card, and the best deliverability of any free tier we've tested. Setup is quick: sign up free, verify a single sender email address by clicking the link it emails you (no domain or DNS records needed), then copy the SMTP username and password it gives you into the fields below. Typical settings: host mail.smtp2go.com, port 587.
2. Already have Gmail? Use an app password instead. No new account needed. Turn on 2-Step Verification if it isn't already, then generate an app password at myaccount.google.com/apppasswords and use that (not your regular Gmail password) below. Settings: host smtp.gmail.com, port 587, username is your full Gmail address.
Save your settings first, then send a test to confirm they work.

Without email: everything else works normally — search, tracking, funnel metrics, and follow-up reminders all still show up in the app. Add email any time under Settings → Email.
