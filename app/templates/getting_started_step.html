AI powers Job Squire's best features: scoring new jobs against your profile, tailoring resumes and cover letters per application, drafting follow-up emails, and weekly strategy reviews. Three ways to get it, in order of privacy:

1. Local AI (most private). Run Ollama on your own machine — your data never leaves it. Needs a reasonably capable computer (16 GB+ memory recommended). Add it under Settings → AI → Providers with provider type "Ollama (local)".
2. Cloud AI with free tiers. Google Gemini, Groq, and OpenRouter all offer free API tiers that work well here. Your identifiers are automatically replaced with placeholders before anything is sent (see Privacy settings). Add keys under Settings → AI → Providers.
3. A Claude subscription (Pro/Max). Connect Claude directly to Job Squire as a live connector — Claude reads your pipeline and writes analyses, kits, and drafts back on its own. Set up under Settings → AI → Connectors. Also have a ChatGPT, Gemini, or Nous subscription? Their API keys work via the Providers tab too.
