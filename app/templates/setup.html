Claude Pro Setup

Automate your job search with Claude

This page walks you through connecting Claude to your Job Squire instance and setting up five automated daily routines. Once configured, Claude will handle your morning briefing, score new job leads, build application kits, draft follow-up emails, and review your weekly strategy automatically. You just review and act.

Step 1

Connect Claude to your Job Squire instance

Claude needs a direct connection to your Job Squire instance to read your pipeline and write results back. This uses the "custom connector" feature in Claude Pro. You only need to do this once.

How to add the connector in Claude Pro

  1. Open claude.ai and sign in to your Pro account.
  2. In the left sidebar, click your profile name, then Settings.
  3. Click Connectors in the settings menu.
  4. Click Add custom connector.
  5. In the Connector URL field, enter:
    {{ public_mcp_url ~ '/mcp' if public_mcp_url else '(set PUBLIC_MCP_URL on the server first)' }} {% if public_mcp_url %} {% endif %}
  6. Give it a name. We suggest: {{ connector }}
  7. Click Connect. A login page will appear — enter your Job Squire instance username and password (the same ones you use to log in here).
  8. Click Authorize. Claude is now connected.
Important: The connector name you choose in step 6 must match the Connector name setting on the Settings page. If you use a different name, update it there so the routine prompts reference it correctly.

Step 2

Set up your 5 daily routines

Claude Pro includes 5 scheduled task slots. Use them for the routines below. Each card has a ready-to-use prompt and step-by-step setup instructions. The routine does all the work and writes results back to your Job Squire instance automatically.

How to create a scheduled routine in Claude Pro
  1. Open claude.ai.
  2. In the left sidebar, look for the clock or calendar icon labeled Scheduled or a similar label. (If you don't see it, make sure you are on a Pro plan.)
  3. Click New scheduled task (or the + button).
  4. Paste the prompt from one of the routine cards below into the message box.
  5. Set the schedule (daily, specific days, specific time).
  6. Give the routine a descriptive name so you can tell them apart.
  7. Click Save. Claude will run it on your schedule from now on.

You can also click "Open in Claude" on any card to run the routine right now and test it before scheduling.

Step 4

Per-job Claude actions

Beyond the daily routines, each job record in your Job Squire instance has direct "Open in Claude" buttons for one-off actions: building an interview prep guide, drafting a follow-up email, or getting an immediate fit score on a specific posting. Look for these buttons on each job's detail page.

These buttons only appear when your connector URL is configured and the AI mode is set to mcp on the Settings page.

Build Application Kit Tailored resume bullets, cover letter, application email, and follow-up emails for one job.
Prep for Interview Likely questions, STAR story mappings from your experience, and things to research.
Draft Follow-Up Email A ready-to-send follow-up email appropriate to where you are in the process.
Score This Job An immediate 1-10 fit score with reasoning, saved back to the job record.
