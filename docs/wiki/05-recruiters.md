# Recruiters and Staffing Agencies

In many markets, a significant share of hiring goes through staffing agencies and recruiters before jobs ever appear on public boards. The **Recruiters** tab is where you track those relationships.

## Adding a recruiter

Click **Recruiters**, then **+ Add contact**. Fill in their name, agency, type (Recruiter, Staffing Agency, Hiring Manager, Networking, or Reference), how to reach them, and a follow-up date.

## Logging a submission

When a recruiter puts you forward for a specific role:

- Open that recruiter and click **+ Log submission**, or
- Click **+ Log submission** directly from the Recruiters list, or
- From a job detail page, scroll to the **Recruiter submissions** section and click **+ Log submission** there.

Record the company, the role, and the date. As things move, update the status: Submitted → Screening → Interviewing → Offer → Placed. If the role is also a tracked job, link it so everything ties together.

## Submission statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Submitted | Recruiter has put you forward; waiting on the client |
| Screening | Initial call or screen scheduled or underway |
| Interviewing | Active interview process with the client |
| Offer | Offer extended |
| Placed | You accepted and started |
| Rejected | Declined by the client |
| Withdrawn | You withdrew |
| No Response | Submission went silent |

## Dashboard surfacing

The dashboard shows:

- **Recruiter follow-ups due** — any contact where the follow-up date is today or earlier.
- **Open submissions** — all submissions in active statuses (Submitted, Screening, Interviewing, Offer).

## Good habits

Every time you talk to a recruiter:
1. Update their **Last contacted** date.
2. Set a new **Follow-up date** before you close the page.

This keeps your dashboard accurate and ensures no relationship goes cold without you noticing.
