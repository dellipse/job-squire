# Quick-Apply Bookmarklet

The bookmarklet lets you capture any job posting in one click without copying and pasting.

## Setup (once)

1. Go to **Settings → AI** tab.
2. Scroll to the **Bookmarklet** section.
3. Drag the **+ Add to JobSquire** button to your browser's bookmarks bar.

Do this once. The bookmarklet is tied to your Job Squire's URL so it opens the right place.

## How to use it

Browse to any job posting on Indeed, LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, or any other site. Click the bookmark in your browser's bar. Your Job Squire opens in a new tab with the job title, company, location, and URL already filled in.

Review the pre-filled details, adjust anything that is wrong or missing, and click Save.

## Auto-extraction support

The bookmarklet automatically extracts the title and company from:

- Indeed
- LinkedIn
- ZipRecruiter

On other sites it falls back to the page title, which you can edit before saving.

## Default status

Jobs captured via the bookmarklet default to **Applied** with today's date, since you are typically capturing a posting you are about to apply to or just applied to. Change the status to **Saved** if you want to review it first before applying.
